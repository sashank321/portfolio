What We Do

We started this team in our first semester because we wanted to do something hands-on. Most of us are from CSE and Mechanical, and we thought it would be cool to actually build something instead of just studying theory. Right now we are working on a go-kart for the BAJA SAE India 2026 competition.

We meet every Saturday at the workshop behind Block C. If you are interested in joining, come talk to any of us or DM us on Instagram.

Our Car — VX-01

This is our first car. We named it VX-01 (Velocity Experimental 01, sounds cool right?). It's still being built but here are the specs we are aiming for:

🏎️ Type Single-seater Go-Kart
⚙️ Engine Honda GX160 (5.5 HP)
🛠️ Frame Tubular Steel (Mild)
⚖️ Weight ~120 kg target
💨 Top Speed 55-60 km/h (est.)
🛑 Brakes Disc (rear)
