Celsious to Farenheit conversion

The formula to convert Celsious to Farenheit

F = (C * 9/5) + 32

Farenheit to Celsious

The formula to convert Farenheit to Celsious

C = (F - 32) * 5/9

Kilogram to Pounds

The formula to convert Kilogram to Pounds

lbs = kg * 2.20462

Pounds to Kilogram

The formula to convert Pounds to Kilogram

kg = lbs / 2.20462
